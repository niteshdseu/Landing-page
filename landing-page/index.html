Delhi, the National Capital Territory (NCT) of India

Delhi, officially known as the National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi, is the capital city of India.

It is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history that dates back thousands of years.

Delhi has been the seat of several powerful empires and is characterized by a unique blend of modernity and tradition.
